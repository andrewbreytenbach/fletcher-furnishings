About Me

I wish I could say I came from a family of woodworkers and sawdust ran in my blood. Instead I am a self-taught woodworker who wants to share my passion for fine woodworking and wooden furniture with you. My wife and I got married in 2015 and and had our first child in 2022 which has been one of the greatest joys in life.

By day and even a lot of nights I am a general surgery resident with hopes of completing a vascular surgery fellowship. Starting in medical school, woodworking has served as a creative outlet and a way to decompress from long and stressful days in the hospital. I strive to bring the same precision, focus, and dedication to my woodworking that is required of me as a future surgeon.
